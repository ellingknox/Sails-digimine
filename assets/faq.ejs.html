Frequently Asked Questions

1
What am I purchasing when Is sign up a mining contract?
A mining contract is a subscription for a certain amount of processing power, usually specified as a hash rate equivalent for a particular coin, e.g. 18Mh/s for Ethereum. This processing power is used to mine cryptocurrency coins, which can be traded for goods, services and money.
2
Are cryptocurrency coins worth anything?
Yes, absolutely they are. Cryptocurrency is a regularly traded digital commodity. You can easily exchange these coins on cryptocurrency exchanges for money.
3
Where can I trade my cryptocurrency coins?
You can trade your cryptocurrency coins on any exchange that offers these servies. There are many online exchanges. Here is a list of a few of the regularly used ones: https://www.coinbase.com , https://poloniex.com/, https://www.kraken.com and https://cex.io/.
4
Am I guaranteed a return on my investment?
Unfortunately, as with most investments there are no guarantees. However, the signs for cryptocurrency going up in price in the future is good. Bitcoin started almost worthless and now is worth over $4000 USD per coin in recent times. Cryptocurrency as a commodity is no different to any other commodity, such as gold or platinum. Like precious metals, they can be mined and then traded for monetray value. Their prices fluctrate and depends on the market supply and demand.
5
Why should I sign up for a mining contract instead of mining myself?
There are many advantages for outsourcing your cryptocurrency mining to a professional company. Firstly, you don't have to manage your mining hardware and software, we optimise our cluster adn mine the most efficient coin automatically, giving you the best possible return on your investment. Secondly, if you don't have the necessary power requirements, hardware space or cooling requirments, then you will be limited to how much you can mine. At DigiMine, we have a dedicated datacentre with all those requirments to provide the most efficient mining possible.
