How It Works

1

DigiMine is a professional crypto currency mining service provider. You purchase a certain amount of processing or hash power from our mining cluster and in return we give you all the returns that hash power producess. We do not take any cuts from your processing power.

2

Customers sign up to a subscription plan, which covers all costs, including hardware purchases, electricity and maintenance.There are no addtional hidden fees.

3

You may choose to mine any coin that's supported by our services. As your host, we will always find the most cost effective way to provide you with your choice. whether that's to directly mine that coin or to mine another coin and exchange it for you on the backend. Either way, you will always get the maximum possible return for the processing power you've purchased.
